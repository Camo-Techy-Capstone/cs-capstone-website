Computer Science Capstone Projects



For over 30 years, Camosun’s Computer Science Department has successfully matched up industry, government and not-for-profit organizations with senior students in their programs to complete a final software project. With newly acquired skills, these students are ready to provide a solution for many business problems and are looking for real-world experience to hone those skills.

Overview

The Capstone process not only offers a meaningful learning experience to our students, but also allows our valued industry partners to tap into the resources and talents of our students. This presents an excellent opportunity for organizations to develop and recruit talented new graduates with company-specific knowledge. For four months, the students will analyze, design and implement a solution for an orgnaization in the community. Projects are completed by the end of August with a public show case. In addition to the sponsor and their team, an instructor in the Computer Science Department serves as an advisor for the student teams.
